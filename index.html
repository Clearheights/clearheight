The Challenge

Traditional Cleaning Is Overdue for Change

High-rise exterior maintenance has remained largely unchanged for decades — expensive, disruptive, and unnecessarily dangerous for the workers involved.

Problem

Traditional scaffolding — Days of rigging, tens of thousands in equipment, and workers exposed to fatal fall hazards at height.

Problem

Rope access — Expensive specialists, weather-dependent, high liability exposure. Limits cleaning frequency significantly.

Our Solution

Drone Cleaning Solutions — One ground operator. The drone reaches every surface. Ionized water cleans without chemicals. No scaffolding, no liability, no delays.
